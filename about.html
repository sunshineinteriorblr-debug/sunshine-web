Who We Are

A Studio Built on Passion & Precision

Sunshine Interiors was founded in 2009 by architect duo Aryan and Meera Kapoor with a single conviction: that thoughtful design has the power to transform not just spaces, but lives. What began as a small Mumbai-based studio has grown into a nationally recognised firm with offices in four cities.

Today our multidisciplinary team of 40+ designers, architects, project managers, and craftspeople brings a breadth of expertise to every engagement — from luxury residences and cutting-edge commercial offices to boutique hotels and flagship retail environments.

We are proud to have delivered over 850 projects across India, earning recognition at the India Interior Design Awards, Architectural Digest Design Show, and Elle Décor India.
