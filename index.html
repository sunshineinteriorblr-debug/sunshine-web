About Sunshine Interiors

Crafting Spaces That Tell Your Story

Founded in 2009, Sunshine Interiors has grown into one of India's most trusted interior design studios. We believe every space holds the potential to inspire — and it is our mission to unlock that potential for each client.

Our team of 40+ designers, architects, and craftspeople collaborate across residential, commercial, and hospitality projects, delivering spaces that are functional, beautiful, and unmistakably personal.
